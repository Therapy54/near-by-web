### Near-By : --Executive Overview--

## Product Summary

    The platform is a real-time global discovery and connection web application that helps people discover nearby and global products, services, jobs, skills, opportunities, and requests.

    The platform does not process payments, logistics, delivery, or financial transactions. Its core purpose is to reduce the distance between human needs and human solutions by enabling fast discovery, communication, and connection.

    ** Users can:

        - Post products they want to sell

        Offer skills and services

        Create job listings

        Search for nearby opportunities

        Discover local and global listings

        Communicate directly through messaging and calls

        Build public professional and personal profiles

        Showcase portfolios and experiences

    The platform acts as a discovery and communication engine rather than a transactional intermediary.

## Vision & Mission

    ** Vision

        To become the world’s leading real-time human discovery network where people can instantly find products, services, jobs, skills, opportunities, and solutions nearby or globally.

    ** Mission

        To simplify human connection by making discovery, communication, and opportunity access instant, local-first, and globally scalable.

## Core Product Philosophy

    ** The Platform DOES

        Connect people

        Surface opportunities

        Enable communication

        Prioritize local relevance

        Simplify discovery

        Provide visibility for individuals and businesses

        Enable real-time interactions

    ** The Platform DOES NOT

        Handle payments

        Process transactions

        Provide escrow

        Handle logistics or delivery

        Manage transportation

        Act as a legal intermediary

        Guarantee listings or services

## Problem Statement

    Modern digital ecosystems are fragmented.

    People currently use different platforms for:

        Jobs

        Selling products

        Freelancing

        Local services

        Rentals

        Social networking

        Professional networking

        Classified listings

        This fragmentation creates:

        Slow discovery

        Poor visibility

        Difficult communication

        Reduced local opportunity access

        Limited trust and connection

        Inefficient searching

        --The platform solves this by creating a unified discovery ecosystem.--
